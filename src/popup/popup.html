Hotkeys (on a camera page)

  • 1–4 switch camera
  • 5 multi-view
  • Q / W / E pan audio left / center / right
  • F fullscreen

Independent, unofficial fan tool — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any network or streaming service. Adds viewing controls to a page you already have access to; collects no data. Use at your own risk.
